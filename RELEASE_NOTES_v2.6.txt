Dispense Intelligence v2.6 - Pending Cycle Lock Fix

- First due date = latest actual dispense date + configured preparation interval.
- An overdue preparation cycle remains on its original due date until staff marks it Dispensed or Others.
- The system no longer skips an unfinished cycle merely because another interval has passed.
- After a cycle is completed, the next due date = actual completion work date + configured interval.
- The interval remains configurable from Admin: 15, 20, 25, or 30 days.
- Scheduling stops at prescription end date.
- Existing Supabase users, roles, workflow records, and audit logs are preserved.

Important: Keep the existing supabase-config.js when uploading.
